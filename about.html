About Kyrillo

Kyrillo is a movie website that enables users to stream and download various movies all for free!

Developed in 2021 by full stack web developer Cyril Asogwa and designed in the same year by ui/ux and front-end web developer Lucy Asogwa, Kyrillo was developed to test the skills of Cyril Asogwa and all this was just done for practice and as a minor project.

Due to minor issues, I had to take down some feaures such as signing up with a profile picture, seeing your contact details displayed on the nav bar and giving you control to edit your details. But these features will be added to my next project and an updated version of this site coming in the nearest future if all goes well.

Int the updated version which will be in works soon, you could expect things link being able to stream the movies live on the site and being able to download a movie live from the site and not being redirected to an external website.
